Nestled among towering redwood trees in the coastal mountains of Sonoma County, Alliance Redwoods Conference Grounds provides the perfect setting for our men's retreat. This beautiful 80-acre property offers a peaceful escape from daily life, creating an ideal environment for spiritual growth, fellowship, and renewal.

Whether you're walking among ancient redwoods, gathering for worship, or sharing meals with brothers in Christ, Alliance Redwoods provides a memorable backdrop for our time together.
